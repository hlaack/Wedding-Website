Q & A

We are sure you have plenty of questions about the wedding, and you've come to the right place.

Look below to find some answers to common questions. Afterwards, if you're still wondering, feel free to reach out.

Who on my invite is invited?

From the envelope wrapping the Save the Date or Invitation you have received, the invited members of your party are strictly those listed on the front.

Concerning children:

This is one of the hardest decisions we had to make. While we love and appreciate everyone's kiddos, we are asking that only those members of your family/party listed on the envelope of either the Save the Date or the Invitation attend.

We completely understand that this requires a great deal of planning and absolutely can be stressful. If this is a barrier to your attendance, we genuinely get it.
