Q & A

We are sure you have plenty of questions about the wedding, and you've come to the right place.

Look below to find some answers to common questions. Afterwards, if you're still wondering, feel free to reach out.

Can I bring a date?

In an effort to keep our guest list intimate, only guests formally named on your invitation or within the RSVP form will be accomodated.

Are children invited?

Only the children whose names are listed specifically on your family's RSVP form are invited. We appreciate your understanding.

What should I wear?

The dress code for our Ceremony and Reception is Cocktail Attire. Suits and ties, formal pantsuits, and elegant, knee-length or midi-length cocktail dresses are all welcome. Please, no denim, khaki, or dresses in shades of white, ivory, or cream.
